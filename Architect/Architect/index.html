Brick Curtain House

It features a gently undulating brick facade that offers shade from the sun. The building’s location at the intersection of two roads so we decided to divide it into two contrasting zones,one encased in concrete and the other in brick. The site is a part of the residential area on a corner plot approached from the road,The layout was organised like the private zone was at the inner side,and public zone at the front.

An exposed brick masonry and terrazzo plaster give the villa an evergreen look.The brick wall with a height of over 30 feet adds to the grandeur of the house. The Parking area is cantilevered, thus offering a rare spacious entry welcoming you to your abode.You feel a sense of spaciousness which gets extended all along your home. As you walk into your home you feel a sense of minute detailing in each and every nook and corner.
